Favorite K-POP Group: BTS

BTS is a 7-member boy band from South Korea under Big Hit Entertainment. Their name comes from the initials of their Korean name, romanized as "BangTan Seonyeondan," which is roughly translated to "Bulletproof Boyscouts." They release music in both Korean and Japanese, and often include short lines of English as well. They are the first Korean group to top Billboard's Social 50 chart, and won the Top Social Artist Award at Billboard Music Awards in 2017, also making them the first Korean group to win a BBMA.
